Pristine Cleaning Services

Reliable, thorough, and professional cleaning tailored to your home.

  • Living Areas: Dusting surfaces, vacuuming carpets, mopping floors, cobweb removal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?

A: No, we bring our own professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly supplies. If you have specific products you'd like us to use, just let us know!


Q: Are you insured and bonded?

A: Yes, absolutely. We carry full liability insurance and bonding for your peace of mind.


Q: What if I'm not happy with the cleaning?

A: We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you spot an issue, call us within 24 hours and we will re-clean the area for free.


Q: Do I need to be home?

A: No. You can leave a key or door code. We are fully insured.


Q: Can I customize the cleaning?

A: Yes! You can add extras like "Oven Clean" or "Window Wash" during booking.
